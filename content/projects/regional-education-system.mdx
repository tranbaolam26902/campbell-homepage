# Regional Education System

A system that can simulate local education lessons in the form of virtual tours, helping students learn about subjects such as the history, geography, traditions, and cultures of the area they live in.

- **Website:** [https://gddplamdong.dlu.edu.vn](https://gddplamdong.dlu.edu.vn)
- **Tech stack:**
- > **Front-end:** ReactJS, Tailwind CSS, Redux, Framer Motion, Panolens.js
- > **Back-end:** ASP\.NET Core, EF Core, SQL Server, JWT
- **Release date:** Q4 2024
